INTRODUCING OUR NEW AI DRUG RESEARCHER

DeepMind's AI program, AlphaFold, has made big improvements in predicting how proteins fold, which is important for understanding how drugs work with their targets.

Atomwise uses AI to study moleculardata and predict which chemicals will work best as medicines. It has already found promising compounds for diseases likeEbola and malaria.

AI has the potential to transform healthcare in numerous ways, from improving diagnostics and treatment to enhancing patient engagement and reducing costs.
